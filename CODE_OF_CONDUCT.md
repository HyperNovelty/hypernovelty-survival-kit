# Code of Conduct

This project is intended for practical public-good work. Participants are expected to be respectful, evidence-aware, and careful about uncertainty.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat people with respect.
- Discuss claims with sources and reasons.
- Make uncertainty visible.
- Keep safety and privacy boundaries clear.
- Assume checklists are aids for judgment, not substitutes for judgment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory conduct.
- Publishing private data, credentials, student records, client material, or sensitive operational details.
- Presenting this educational material as professional legal, medical, financial, emergency, or safety advice.
- Using the project to evade accountability or human review.

Maintainers may remove contributions or participation that conflict with these expectations.
