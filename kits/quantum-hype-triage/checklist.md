# Quantum Hype Triage Checklist

Use this when a product, article, vendor, or investment claim leans on quantum language.

- What specific quantum area is being discussed?
- Is the claim about research, prototype, production system, security migration, or metaphor?
- What capability is measurable today?
- What assumptions are required for the claim to become practical?
- Are timelines tied to hardware, error correction, cost, standards, or specialist labor?
- Is there an independent technical source?
- What decision would this claim affect?
- Who has the expertise to review it?

Human gate: do not use this checklist alone for procurement, investment, cybersecurity, or curriculum decisions.
