# Climate Signal Checklist

Use this to separate local adaptation signals from broad claims.

- What place does the decision concern?
- What time horizon matters: today, season, year, decade, or infrastructure lifetime?
- Is the evidence local observation, official forecast, model projection, insurance data, infrastructure data, or anecdote?
- What decision is being considered?
- What would happen if the signal is wrong or outdated?
- Who is most exposed if the decision fails?
- Which local expert, agency, or qualified professional should review the decision?
- When should the decision be revisited?

Boundary: this checklist is not emergency guidance. Follow emergency services and local officials during immediate hazards.
