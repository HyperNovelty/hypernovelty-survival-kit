# School AI Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist to start a practical conversation. It is not a complete policy.

- Name the learning goals where AI may help.
- Name the learning goals where AI use would defeat the assignment.
- Define what students must disclose about AI assistance.
- Protect student privacy and avoid entering identifiable records into unapproved tools.
- Provide at least one AI-free path for students who cannot or should not use a tool.
- Ask students to show process, notes, drafts, or oral explanation when appropriate.
- Train staff on source checking and hallucination risk.
- Set a human review gate for discipline, grading disputes, accommodations, and student safety concerns.

Boundary: this checklist is educational. Follow local law, school policy, and qualified guidance for formal decisions.
