# Small-Business AI Workflow Safety Checklist

Use this before adding AI to routine business work.

- List the workflow and the business decision it affects.
- Identify private, customer, financial, employee, or regulated data that must not be entered into tools.
- Decide who reviews AI output before it reaches customers, vendors, employees, or public channels.
- Keep humans responsible for pricing, contracts, hiring, firing, refunds, safety issues, and financial commitments.
- Test on low-stakes examples before production use.
- Save prompts, outputs, and review notes for consequential work.
- Check vendor terms, data retention, and account access.
- Create a stop rule for errors, complaints, or unexpected tool behavior.

Boundary: this checklist is not legal, financial, cybersecurity, or compliance advice. Use qualified professionals for high-stakes decisions.
