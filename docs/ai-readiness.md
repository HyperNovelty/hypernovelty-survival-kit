# AI Readiness

AI readiness means being able to use AI tools without confusing fluency for accuracy or automation for accountability.

## Practical Checks

- Identify the task and the stakes before choosing a tool.
- Decide what information must not be entered into an AI system.
- Require sources for factual claims that matter.
- Use a human review gate before publishing, sending, buying, hiring, firing, diagnosing, or making legal or financial decisions.
- Keep an audit trail for important AI-assisted work.

## Watch For

- Confident but unsupported claims.
- Hidden privacy exposure.
- A tool being used because it is available, not because it is appropriate.
- People accepting AI output because review feels slower than action.

## Human Gate

A named person should review high-stakes output and be able to explain what changed because of that review.
