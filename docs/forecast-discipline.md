# Forecast Discipline

Forecast discipline means turning uncertainty into trackable claims instead of vibes, slogans, or panic.

## Practical Checks

- State the claim in a way that can be checked later.
- Include a time horizon.
- Use probabilities or confidence levels where useful.
- Name the evidence that would change your mind.
- Review old forecasts and update without hiding misses.

## Watch For

- Predictions with no date.
- Certainty language without evidence.
- Forecasts that cannot be falsified.
- Changing definitions after the fact.

## Human Gate

For important choices, a reviewer should check whether the forecast is specific, time-bound, and connected to a decision.
