# HyperNovelty OSS Portfolio Map

This is the public map for the HyperNovelty Open Lab repository set. The projects are intentionally small, local-first, and review-oriented. They are meant to make AI-era work easier to inspect, not to claim certification, compliance coverage, or autonomous authority.

## Portfolio thesis

HyperNovelty OSS focuses on a practical question:

> When AI, media, education, climate, work, and institutions are changing quickly, what lightweight public tools help people slow down, check sources, document agent activity, and keep human review visible?

## What these repos have in common

- Public-safe fixtures and synthetic examples.
- Local-first validators or review artifacts where possible.
- Explicit boundaries around secrets, private data, and account actions.
- Human review gates rather than autonomous public action.
- Small contribution surfaces suitable for documentation, examples, and validators.

## What these repos do not claim

They do not provide legal, medical, financial, safety, compliance, school-policy, or emergency advice. They do not certify workflows as safe. They do not authorize agents to publish, spend money, contact people, or act on accounts.

## Near-term maintainer roadmap

- Add CI validation to all public repos.
- Add issue templates and good-first-issue surfaces.
- Publish clear synthetic examples and rendered demos.
- Package the most reusable validators as installable tools only after review.
- Keep external contributions small, reviewable, and public-safe.
