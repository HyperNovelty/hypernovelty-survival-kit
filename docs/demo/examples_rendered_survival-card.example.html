Require a Human Review Gate for Student-Facing AI Output

Synthetic example Human gate required Local review aid
Card ID
school-ai-readiness-001
Domain
education-readiness
Audience
Teachers, school leaders, and parent advisory groups

Problem

AI tools can produce fluent explanations, lesson materials, or student feedback that contain errors, bias, privacy exposure, or claims that do not match the learning goal.

Practical Step

Before AI-assisted material reaches students or families, assign a named adult reviewer to check accuracy, age fit, privacy exposure, and alignment with the learning goal.

Human Gate

A teacher or school leader signs off before the material is used, and records what was changed or rejected after review.

Caveat

This card is a public education aid. It is not legal advice, student safety advice, or a substitute for local school policy and qualified professional judgment.
