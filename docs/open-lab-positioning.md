# Open Lab Positioning

This repository is the umbrella map for the six-repo Hypernovelty Open Lab public proof footprint. It keeps the practical question visible: what small review habit, checklist, or human gate helps people stay oriented when fast-changing systems make old assumptions brittle?

## Role In The Footprint

- `hypernovelty-survival-kit`: umbrella map, domain notes, and survival-card starter.
- `agent-run-receipts`: accountability receipts for bounded agent-assisted work.
- `ai-workflow-safety-screen`: public-safe workflow intake screen for deciding when AI use needs human review.
- `source-card-schema`: structured source, claim, evidence, limitation, and review-state cards.
- `hypernovelty-verification-literacy-kit`: teachable mini-labs for verification habits.
- `ai-school-readiness-kit`: school and learning-program AI readiness review kit.

## Public-Safe Boundary

The footprint is educational and prototype-oriented. It is not a certified compliance system, legal opinion, cybersecurity program, student-safety policy, or operational approval flow. Examples should stay synthetic or public-safe, and any real use needs local review by qualified humans.
