# Education Readiness

Education readiness means protecting learning, privacy, and teacher judgment while students and schools adapt to AI and other fast-changing tools.

## Practical Checks

- Define the learning goal before deciding whether AI use is allowed.
- Separate help, practice, drafting, tutoring, and replacement work.
- Protect student data and avoid entering identifiable records into tools without approved policy.
- Ask students to explain process, not just submit a product.
- Keep parents, teachers, and administrators aligned on review expectations.

## Watch For

- Policies that punish without teaching.
- Tools that make assignments easier to complete but harder to learn from.
- Surveillance-heavy approaches that damage trust.

## Human Gate

Teachers and school leaders should decide where AI supports learning and where it undermines the purpose of an assignment.
