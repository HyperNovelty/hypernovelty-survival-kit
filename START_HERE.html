Hypernovelty Survival Kit

Plain checklists and survival cards for staying oriented when change moves faster than normal playbooks can update.

Domain Map

AI readinessUse AI with source checks, human review, and clear limits.
Agent workflowsKeep automated work observable, reversible, and accountable.
EducationProtect learning goals, student privacy, and teacher judgment.
Media provenanceAsk where a claim came from and how it changed.
Climate signalsSeparate local adaptation signals from broad trend claims.
Quantum literacySort useful quantum claims from hype and theater.
Forecast disciplineName uncertainty, time horizons, and update triggers.
Memory governanceDecide what systems may remember, forget, or expose.

Quick Start

Read one domain note, choose one checklist, then adapt the example survival card. Keep the card small enough that another person can review it before it is used.

Validate a card with python3 scripts/validate_survival_card.py examples/survival-card.example.json.

Render the browser demo with python3 scripts/render_survival_card_html.py examples/survival-card.example.json examples/rendered/survival-card.example.html.
